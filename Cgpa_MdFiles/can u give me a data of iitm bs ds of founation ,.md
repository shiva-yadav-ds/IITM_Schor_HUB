### **KEY POINTS TO REMEMBER:**

1. **Total Courses:** 31+ courses (excluding projects)
2. **Foundation Level:** All 8 courses (32 credits) must be completed before starting Diploma
3. **Diploma Level:** Both tracks can be done - you can choose one or both diplomas
4. **Time to Complete:** 4-8 years depending on pace (recommended 10 hours/week per course)
5. **Flexibility:** You can exit at any level with corresponding certifications
6. **Total Credits for Full Degree (BS):** 142 credits
7. **Total Credits for Intermediate Degree (BSc):** 114 credits

Each level requires approximately **1-3 years** to complete depending on how many courses you take per term (maximum 4 courses per term).

Since you're already in the Foundation level of IITM BS Data Science program, you're following exactly this structure! The progression is mandatory in sequence - you cannot skip levels, but you have flexibility in the pace at which you complete each level.
